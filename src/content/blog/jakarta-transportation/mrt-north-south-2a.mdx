# MRT North-South Line (Fase 2A)

The **MRT North-South Line (Fase 2A)** is the first extension of Jakarta's MRT system, currently under construction to extend the network further into North Jakarta.

## Project Information

**Line Code**: NS2A (Red)  
**Status**: Under Construction  
**Route**: Bundaran HI - Kota  
**Expected Completion**: TBA

## Key Features

- Extension of the operational Fase 1 line
- Mostly underground construction
- Will serve central business and historical districts
- Critical for connecting old city area with modern metro

## Planned Stations

The extension will add stations including:

- Bundaran HI (connection with Fase 1)
- Harmoni
- Sawah Besar
- Mangga Besar
- Glodok
- Kota (northern terminus)

## Strategic Importance

This extension will:

- Connect the historic old town (Kota) area
- Serve densely populated central districts
- Improve access to business areas
- Reduce traffic in congested northern corridors
- Support tourism to heritage sites

## Construction Challenges

The project faces:

- Underground construction in dense urban areas
- Preservation of historical sites
- Utility relocation
- Minimizing disruption to existing businesses

## Economic Impact

Expected benefits include:

- Increased property values along the route
- Improved business accessibility
- Reduced commute times
- Enhanced tourism infrastructure
- Job creation during and after construction

## Integration

Will seamlessly connect with:

- MRT Fase 1 for through-running
- KRL lines at Kota
- TransJakarta network
- Future MRT extensions

## Tourism Boost

The extension will improve access to:

- Kota Tua (Old Town) heritage area
- Museums and historical sites
- Glodok Chinatown
- Colonial architecture
- Cultural attractions

Once completed, Fase 2A will make Jakarta's MRT network significantly more useful for both daily commuters and tourists.
