# MRT East-West Line (Fase 3)

The **MRT East-West Line (Fase 3)** is a planned new MRT line that will run perpendicular to the North-South line, creating a comprehensive metro network for Jakarta.

## Project Information

**Line Code**: EW3 (Blue)  
**Status**: Under Construction/Planning  
**Route**: Kalideres (West) - Cikarang (East)  
**Expected Completion**: TBA

## Key Features

- Completely new line crossing Jakarta east-west
- Will intersect with North-South line
- Serves previously underserved corridors
- Creates true metro network with transfers

## Planned Route

The line will cross Jakarta:

- Starting from western areas (Kalideres)
- Passing through central business district
- Continuing to eastern regions
- Potentially extending to Cikarang

## Network Integration

This line will:

- Create critical transfer points with NS line
- Connect with KRL at multiple stations
- Form the backbone of Jakarta's metro grid
- Enable cross-city trips without transfers through center

## Strategic Importance

The East-West line is crucial for:

- Reducing north-south traffic bottlenecks
- Serving east-west commuting patterns
- Completing a true metro network
- Decentralizing traffic flows
- Supporting polycentric development

## Economic Corridors

Will serve important areas:

- Western Jakarta industrial zones
- Central business districts
- Eastern residential and industrial areas
- Major commercial centers
- Employment hubs

## Development Impact

Expected to:

- Transform urban development patterns
- Create new commercial centers
- Reduce dependence on private vehicles
- Enable higher-density development
- Support sustainable urban growth

## Regional Significance

The line represents:

- Commitment to comprehensive metro network
- Long-term vision for Jakarta's transit
- Regional coordination and planning
- Modern metropolitan infrastructure
- Sustainable transportation solution

## Transfer Stations

Will feature:

- Major interchange with North-South line
- Connections to KRL network
- Integration with TransJakarta
- Modern transfer facilities
- Seamless multi-modal connections

Once completed, the East-West line will fundamentally change how Jakartans move through their city, creating a true world-class metro system.
