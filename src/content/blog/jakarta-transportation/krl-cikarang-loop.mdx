# Cikarang Loop Line

The **Cikarang Loop Line** (Blue Line) is the most extensive KRL network, forming a loop that connects Jakarta with the eastern industrial regions including Bekasi and Cikarang.

## Route Information

**Line Code**: C (Blue)  
**Status**: Operational  
**Type**: Loop Line (Multiple interconnected routes)

## Key Features

- Largest KRL network with multiple interconnected segments
- Serves major industrial areas in Bekasi and Cikarang
- Provides crucial transportation for factory workers and residents
- Forms a complete loop allowing circular travel patterns

## Coverage Areas

The Cikarang Loop connects several important areas:

- Jakarta city center
- Bekasi residential and industrial zones
- Cikarang industrial estate
- Eastern Jakarta suburbs

## Service Patterns

Due to its loop configuration, this line offers:

- Multiple routing options
- Frequent service on all segments
- Direct connections to employment centers
- Peak hour express services

## Major Interchange Points

Connects with other lines at multiple strategic locations, making it a backbone of the Jakarta commuter rail system.

## Economic Impact

This line plays a vital role in Jakarta's economy by:

- Connecting workers to industrial areas
- Reducing road congestion
- Supporting urban development in eastern corridors
