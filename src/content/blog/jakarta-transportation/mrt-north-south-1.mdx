# MRT North-South Line (Fase 1)

The **MRT North-South Line (Fase 1)** is Jakarta's first operational metro line, representing a major milestone in the city's public transportation infrastructure.

## Route Information

**Line Code**: NS1 (Red)  
**Status**: Operational since 2019  
**Route**: Lebak Bulus - Bundaran HI

## Key Features

- Jakarta's first modern metro system
- Fully air-conditioned trains
- Grade-separated (elevated and underground sections)
- Modern stations with accessibility features
- Electronic ticketing system

## Major Stations

The line serves 13 stations including:

- Lebak Bulus Grab (southern terminus)
- Fatmawati
- Cipete Raya
- Blok M
- ASEAN
- Senayan
- Istora
- Bendungan Hilir
- Setiabudi
- Dukuh Atas BNI (major interchange)
- Bundaran HI (northern terminus)

## Service Information

- Operating hours: ~5:00 AM to midnight
- Headway: 5-10 minutes during peak hours
- Fast and reliable service
- Real-time arrival information

## Technology & Design

Features modern metro technology:

- Automatic train operation
- Platform screen doors
- CCTV security systems
- Disabled-accessible facilities
- Modern architectural design

## Impact on Jakarta

The MRT has:

- Reduced travel times significantly
- Decreased road traffic congestion
- Set new standards for public transport
- Encouraged transit-oriented development
- Improved air quality

## Connections

Integrates with:

- KRL at Dukuh Atas
- TransJakarta at multiple stations
- Future MRT extensions
- Airport train at Dukuh Atas

## Future Connection

This operational phase will connect with Fase 2A and 2B extensions currently under construction, eventually reaching Kota area in North Jakarta.
