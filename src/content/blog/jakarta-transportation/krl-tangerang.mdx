# Tangerang Line

The **Tangerang Line** (Brown Line) connects Jakarta with Tangerang city, serving the western corridor of the metropolitan area.

## Route Information

**Line Code**: T (Brown)  
**Status**: Operational  
**Route**: Duri - Tangerang

## Key Features

- Serves the growing population of Tangerang city
- Connects western Jakarta with the city center
- Important for commuters from Tangerang's residential areas
- Provides alternative to congested highway routes

## Major Stations

Important stations along the line:

- Duri (interchange with other KRL lines)
- Tanah Abang
- Tangerang city stations
- Multiple suburban stops

## Service Patterns

The line provides:

- Regular service throughout the day
- Increased frequency during peak commuting hours
- Direct connections to Jakarta's business districts
- Efficient travel times compared to road transport

## Development Impact

The Tangerang Line has:

- Spurred residential development along its route
- Reduced traffic congestion on western access roads
- Made commuting more affordable and predictable
- Connected Tangerang's urban areas with Jakarta's economy

## Future Expansion

Plans for extension and improvement continue as Tangerang's population grows and urbanization expands westward.

## Connections

Interchanges with:

- Other KRL lines at Duri and Tanah Abang
- TransJakarta bus network
- Local transportation systems
