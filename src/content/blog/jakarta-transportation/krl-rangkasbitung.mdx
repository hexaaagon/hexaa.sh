# Rangkasbitung Line

The **Rangkasbitung Line** (Green Line) is one of the major KRL Commuter Lines serving the Jakarta metropolitan area, connecting Jakarta with Rangkasbitung in Banten province.

## Route Information

**Line Code**: R (Green)  
**Status**: Operational  
**Route**: Jakarta Kota - Tanah Abang - Rangkasbitung

## Key Features

- Serves commuters from Banten province into central Jakarta
- Connects with other KRL lines at major interchange stations
- Provides access to business districts and residential areas

## Major Stations

The line passes through several important stations including:

- Jakarta Kota
- Tanah Abang
- Duri
- Kebayoran
- And many more suburban stations

## Service Information

The Rangkasbitung Line operates throughout the day with frequent service during peak hours, serving thousands of commuters traveling between Jakarta and the western suburbs.

## Connections

This line connects with:

- Other KRL lines at Jakarta Kota and Tanah Abang
- Bus rapid transit (TransJakarta) at various stations
- Local transportation at all stations
