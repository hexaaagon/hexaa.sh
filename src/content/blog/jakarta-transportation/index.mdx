# Jakarta Transportation System

Welcome to the interactive guide for Jakarta's rail transportation network. Jakarta's public transit system consists of two main rail networks that serve millions of passengers daily.

## Overview

Jakarta's rail network includes:

- **KRL Commuter Lines**: Five major lines connecting Jakarta with surrounding cities
- **MRT Jakarta**: Modern rapid transit system with operational and under-construction lines

### KRL Commuter Lines

The KRL (Kereta Rel Listrik) is an electric commuter rail system that connects Jakarta with its satellite cities. The network consists of:

- **Rangkasbitung Line** (Green)
- **Cikarang Loop Line** (Blue)
- **Bogor Line** (Red)
- **Tangerang Line** (Brown)
- **Tanjung Priok Line** (Pink)

### MRT Jakarta

The Mass Rapid Transit (MRT) is Jakarta's modern metro system, with both operational and under-construction lines:

- **MRT North-South (Fase 1)** - Operational
- **MRT North-South (Fase 2A)** - Under Construction
- **MRT North-South (Fase 2B)** - Under Construction
- **MRT East-West (Fase 3)** - Under Construction
