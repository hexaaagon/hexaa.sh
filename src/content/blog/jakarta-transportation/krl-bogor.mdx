# Bogor Line

The **Bogor Line** (Red Line) is one of the busiest and most important KRL commuter lines, connecting Jakarta with the city of Bogor in West Java.

## Route Information

**Line Code**: B (Red)  
**Status**: Operational  
**Route**: Jakarta Kota - Bogor / Nambo

## Key Features

- One of the oldest and busiest commuter rail lines
- Extremely high passenger volume during peak hours
- Multiple branches serving different destinations
- Critical for daily commuters from Bogor and Depok

## Major Stations

Key stations along the route include:

- Jakarta Kota
- Manggarai (major interchange)
- Cikini
- Gondangdia
- Sudirman
- Depok
- Bogor

## Service Information

This line is known for:

- Very frequent service (trains every few minutes during peak hours)
- Express and local service patterns
- High-capacity trains to handle passenger volume
- Extended operating hours

## Passenger Demographics

The Bogor Line primarily serves:

- Office workers commuting to Jakarta
- Students traveling to educational institutions
- Residents of Depok and Bogor
- Tourists visiting Bogor's attractions

## Historical Significance

One of the oldest railway lines in Indonesia, with history dating back to the colonial era. The line has been continuously modernized to meet growing demand.

## Connections

Major interchanges with:

- Other KRL lines at Manggarai and Jakarta Kota
- MRT at Sudirman station
- TransJakarta and local buses at all stations
