# Tanjung Priok Line

The **Tanjung Priok Line** (Pink Line) serves Jakarta's northern region, connecting the important port area of Tanjung Priok with central Jakarta.

## Route Information

**Line Code**: TP (Pink)  
**Status**: Operational  
**Route**: Jakarta Kota - Tanjung Priok - Ancol

## Key Features

- Serves Jakarta's northern coastal area
- Connects the busy port district with city center
- Provides access to coastal recreational areas
- Important for port workers and northern residents

## Major Stations

Key stations include:

- Jakarta Kota
- Kampung Bandan
- Ancol (near recreational areas)
- Tanjung Priok

## Service Information

The Tanjung Priok Line offers:

- Regular daily service
- Peak hour frequency increases
- Connections to northern Jakarta's industrial and port areas
- Access to coastal tourist destinations

## Areas Served

This line is crucial for:

- Port and logistics workers
- Residents of northern Jakarta
- Visitors to Ancol recreation area
- Industrial zone employees

## Strategic Importance

The line plays a key role in:

- Reducing port area traffic congestion
- Connecting maritime commerce with Jakarta
- Providing affordable transportation to northern neighborhoods
- Supporting tourism to coastal areas

## Tourist Access

The line provides convenient access to:

- Ancol Dreamland
- Beach areas
- Maritime museums
- Port area attractions

## Connections

Links with:

- Other KRL lines at Jakarta Kota
- Local buses and angkot
- Port area transportation
- Ancol area shuttles
